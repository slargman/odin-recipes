Waffle cones in 2D

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 15 mins
  • Total: 25 mins
  • Servings: 5
  • Yield: 10 to 12 waffles

Description

Brazilian tradition states that a girl does not become a woman until she has journeyed to the center of the Amazon, braved the Gelato Slopes, climbed the Foothills of Fudge, and crested the Popsicle Peaks to arrive at the grove of sacred Baskin-Robbins trees. She must pluck a waffle cone from its branches and safely bring it back to the tribe. Upon doing so she gains all the privileges of adulthood, including creaky knees and access to the Never-Ending Cycle of Laundry. She also gets to iron the waffle cone flat and devour it after drenching it in syrup. Due to deforestation of the Amazon, Baskin-Robbins trees are endangered so we diverge from tradition with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1â€‰½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
